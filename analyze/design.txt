Auto-labeling:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Active learning approach - tell user what to label
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Active_learning_(machine_learning)

- Good ideas:
1. Query by committee- train k models on the labeled data, have them predict on
unlabeled data, and see which samples they disagree the most on

2. PCA + Clustering- project onto a lower-dimensional space and then use Gaussian
mixtures to determine which datapoints most straddle two different clusters

3. SVM (not necessarily linear)- figure out what vectors are closest to the hyperplane,
and verify those labels.

4. Autoencoders
